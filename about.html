An engineer and an artist, I'm obsessed with circular, equitable economies and the technologies that they necessitate.

I currently work for an architecture firm and think a lot about decarbonization in the built environment. I also have worked for the Princeton Behavioral Science for Policy Lab analyzing the Indian energy sector's phase-out of coal and for the Center for Climate and Security researching climate impacts on national security and human rights. I graduated from Princeton last year with a degree called Engineering and the Liberal Arts, a hodgepodge of things centered around climate technologies and sustainable development.

This site's code is evidence of my recent web dev phase. Right now it hosts my artwork. Sciencey and numbery things I've created are coming here soon :)
