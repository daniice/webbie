An engineer and an artist, I'm obsessed with circular, equitable economies and the technologies that they necessitate. I strive to find connections between diciplines that create harmony amongst humans. The words and images on this site are part of this effort.

I currently work for an architecture firm and think a lot about the decarbonization of the built environment. I also have worked for the Princeton Behavioral Science for Policy lab analyzing the Indian energy sector's phase-out of coal and the Center for Climate and Security researching climate impacts on national security and human rights. I graduated from Princeton last year with a degree called Engineering in the Liberal Arts, a hodgepodge of things centered around climate technologies and sustainable development.

This site's code is evidence of my recent web development phase. Sciencey and numbery things I've created are coming here soon :)
